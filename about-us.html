About Us

Our Story

Who We Are

Established in 2012, Trilogy Floral is one of the leading growers, importers, and distributors of fresh cut flowers in North America. What began as a small operation with a big vision has grown into a trusted source for wholesale florists from coast to coast — built on a foundation of quality, consistency, and long-term relationships.

An Innovative Approach

We’re more than a distributor. Trilogy Floral owns and partners with farms across Colombia and Ecuador, giving us direct control over the varieties we grow, how they’re harvested, and how quickly they reach our Miami hub. This farm-to-florist model is how we deliver premium quality at competitive prices — without the unpredictability that comes with traditional supply chains.

Every stem we ship has been carefully selected, graded, and handled with the care it deserves. From classic red roses to the latest tinted specialty varieties, our catalog reflects what today’s designers actually want to work with.

We’re proud of what we’ve built — and even more excited about what’s ahead.

Partnering for Growth

In today’s rapidly changing floral industry, the most valuable thing we can offer our partners is a way to stand out against their competition — through quality, reliability, and variety that nobody else can match.

Year-Round Availability

We pride ourselves on building mutually beneficial partnerships with wholesale florists. By working with the country’s best distributors, we deliver our finest quality flowers to every corner of North America — from boutique florists in New York to mass-market retailers in California.

Our farms and production operations are structured for consistent, year-round availability of the most popular varieties. Whether it’s February 14th or the Tuesday after Labor Day, the flowers you count on are the flowers we ship.

And because we control our supply from farm to warehouse, we can move quickly when the market shifts — introducing new colors, new varieties, and new specialty products ahead of the curve.

Our Facilities

Every Trilogy Floral shipment moves through our Miami distribution hub — a world-class cold-chain operation built for speed, freshness, and scale.

300,000 sq ft — Steps From MIA

Trilogy Floral partners with Flora Logistics at their Miami Distribution Center, minutes from Miami International Airport. The 300,000-square-foot facility features state-of-the-art refrigerated storage, pre-cooling rooms, and high-throughput shipping lanes — engineered specifically for fresh cut flowers.

Flowers arrive from Colombia and Ecuador within hours of being cut, pass through temperature-controlled inspection and grading, and go out the door the same day to florists nationwide. The result: stems that reach your shop with maximum vase life and minimum handling.
