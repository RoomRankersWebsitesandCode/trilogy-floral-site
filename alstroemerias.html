Alstroemerias

The Alstroemeria Perfection is the only variety that reveals its true color the moment it arrives — a standout in a category where so many blooms need time to open. Representing just 5% of the world’s alstroemeria production and 14% of Colombia’s, Perfection delivers blooms up to 25% larger than a regular alstroemeria, on 85 cm stems that measure 7 mm thick, with five to seven flowers per stem. The result is a longer, sturdier, more colorful alstroemeria that holds its own in any arrangement.
