L.A. Lilies

L.A. Lilies are a cross between Longiflorum and Asiatic lilies, combining the elegant trumpet shape of the Longiflorum with the rich, diverse colors of the Asiatic. They offer large, upward-facing blooms with excellent vase life and little to no fragrance — making them versatile for any setting.
